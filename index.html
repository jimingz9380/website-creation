Intro

The Merbear is a new species of bear that is quite unique compared to the other types of bears. It became a new species shortly after the polar bears went extinct. Studies have shown that as the Arctic melted, polar bears slowly started disappearing till it under-go Natural Selection from a random mutation which allows a bear to be born with fins better for surviving underwater. As time went on more of the polar bears took the features of a fish allowing it to have a slimmer body, less fur, back legs turned into fins, and gills. This allowed the bears to fully live underwater and use their front paws which still exist as good tools for hunting.
